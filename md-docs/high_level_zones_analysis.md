## Level Scaling Guidelines

When integrating tbadoc zones into current:

1. **Basic Conversion**: `current_level = tbadoc_level √ó 3.33`
2. **High-Level Zones**: Scale max level to 100 (not 133) for game balance
3. **Boss Mobs**: Scale to level 100 for epic encounters
4. **Regular Mobs**: Scale proportionally within zone's level range
5. **Loot**: Adjust gold/exp rewards for current economy

**Example Scaling**:
- tbadoc mob level 20 ‚Üí current level 66
- tbadoc mob level 25 ‚Üí current level 83  
- tbadoc mob level 30 ‚Üí current level 100 (cap)
- tbadoc mob level 34+ ‚Üí current level 100 (cap, adjust HP/damage instead)
